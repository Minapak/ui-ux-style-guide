01 Skeuomorphism Design that imitates real-world objects — leather, metal and paper textures with 3D buttons make the screen feel touchable.
02 Neumorphism A soft UI where elements look gently ‘pushed in’ or ‘extruded’ from a same-color background. Dual-direction shadows are the whole trick.
03 Glassmorphism A frosted-glass effect where a blurred background shows through translucent panels — delivering depth and polish at once.
04 Claymorphism A soft-clay 3D look — chunky rounded shapes with inner and outer shadows create a puffy, friendly feel.
05 Minimalism ‘Less is the design’ — whitespace, restrained color and typography focus attention on content. The most durable approach.
06 Maximalism ‘More is more’ — bold color, huge type and overlapping elements explode with personality. Minimalism’s opposite.
07 Brutalism Raw, unpolished web — default fonts, harsh borders and stark contrast that break the rules on purpose.
08 Liquid Glass Apple’s 2025 material — reflects and refracts light like glass while flowing like liquid. The evolution of glassmorphism.
09 Bento Grid A modular grid that packs information into differently-sized cells like a Japanese bento box — popularized by Apple keynotes, a core 2026 layout.
10 Spatial UI An interface placed in depth and 3D space, beyond the flat plane — the design language of spatial computing led by Apple Vision Pro (visionOS).
11 Flat Design No shadows, gradients or textures — bold solid color, crisp edges and simple shapes. The reaction to skeuomorphism and the base of most modern UI.
12 Material Design 3 Comprehensive, accessible Google system with dynamic color and cross-platform libraries; opinionated and heavier to adopt.
13 Neo-Brutalism Bold, edgy web style with hard borders/shadows and clashing color; great for launches, risky for enterprise.
14 Swiss Style Objective, typography-led design on strict mathematical grids with sans-serif type and a spare red/black/white palette.
15 Dark Mode Dark surfaces with light text — elevation by lightness, desaturated accents and AA-tuned contrast.
16 Aurora Gradient Blurred multi-color mesh gradients glowing behind minimal typography — the signature SaaS/AI hero look.
17 Y2K / Frutiger Aero Glossy skeuomorphic aqua revival — droplets, bubbles, chrome and lens flare; playful and nostalgic.
18 Memphis Playful 80s decorative style — clashing color, squiggles, zigzags and polka-dot confetti framing clean content.
19 Retro / Pixel Blocky low-res 8-bit game UI — pixel fonts, hard edges, limited palettes and chunky bordered panels.

All metrics are relative references as of July 13, 2026. Higher ‘Effort’ means greater development cost.
